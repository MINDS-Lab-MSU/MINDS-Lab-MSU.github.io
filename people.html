Brief Biography

Dr. Jamell Dacon is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Morgan State University as of September 2023, and the Principal Investigator of the MINDS Lab. He earned his Ph.D. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science from Michigan State University (MSU) in 2023 and 2020, respectively, while under the supervision of Dr. Jiliang Tang. Before that, he earned his B.S. degree in Mathematics and A.S. degree in Computer Science from the City University of New York—Medgar Evers College in 2018. His primary research spans the areas of Trustworthy AI, Natural Language Processing, Computational Social Science, and Interdisciplinary Data Science. Moreover, he regularly serves as PC member and reviewer for multiple top-tier international conferences and journals in machine learning and data science such as ACL, ACL-IJCNLP, NAACL, LREC, AAAI, KDD, TheWebConf, and IJCAI.
